## 4. PROJECTS PAGE

Display projects in large cards with images and impact highlights.

### Community Clean-Up Drive

(Collaboration with BPT Seva)

Urban Poona Impact Club organized a large-scale community clean-up initiative in collaboration with BPT Seva to promote environmental responsibility and improve local public spaces.

More than 20 volunteers participated in the initiative and collectively removed over 300 kilograms of waste from public areas. The team successfully cleaned approximately one square kilometre of space, restoring cleanliness and raising awareness about responsible waste management.

The initiative also helped educate nearby residents about environmental sustainability and the importance of maintaining clean public environments.

Impact Highlights:

* 300+ kilograms of waste collected
* 20+ volunteers participated
* 1 square kilometre area cleaned

---

### UPBFL – Urban Poona Basketball Fundraiser League

Urban Poona organized the Urban Poona Basketball Fundraiser League (UPBFL), a community sports initiative designed to raise funds for GRIP Charitable Foundation.

The event brought together more than 20 basketball teams and created a platform where athletes and volunteers could participate in a competitive tournament while supporting a charitable cause.

The tournament demonstrated how sports can unite communities and raise awareness for social initiatives while generating financial support for charitable organizations.

Impact Highlights:

* 20+ teams participated
* Successful fundraising event
* Community engagement through sports

---

### Visit to RESQ Charitable Trust

Urban Poona volunteers visited RESQ Charitable Trust to learn about wildlife rescue operations, animal rehabilitation, and conservation efforts.

During the visit, members gained valuable insights into the challenges faced in rescuing injured animals and providing medical care for wildlife.

The experience helped increase awareness about animal welfare and inspired volunteers to support wildlife rescue initiatives.

---

### Visit to GRIP Charitable Foundation

Members of Urban Poona also visited GRIP Charitable Foundation to learn about its work in social welfare and community development.

The visit helped volunteers understand how charitable organizations operate and how collaborative efforts between NGOs and volunteers can create sustainable social impact.

This experience strengthened Urban Poona’s commitment to working with organizations that aim to improve communities.
